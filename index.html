Meend (Glide)

A smooth, continuous slide between two notes without breaking the sound. This creates a flowing connection between pitches and is essential for expressing the emotional content of ragas.

Sa →→→→→→→→→ Pa

Click and drag along the string to experience Meend

Kan (Grace Note)

A quick, light touch of an adjacent note before reaching the main note. It adds subtle color and helps define the character of specific ragas.

Re Ga

Click the grace note then the main note to hear Kan

Krintan (Shake)

A rapid oscillation or vibrato on a single note, creating a trembling effect. This technique adds intensity and emotional depth to sustained notes.

Ma ≈≈≈≈≈≈≈≈≈

Click and hold on the string to experience Krintan

Sparsh (Touch)

A brief, gentle touching of a note, often used to approach the main note from above or below. It's more subtle than a full grace note.

Dha Ni

Tap lightly on the touch note then the main note

Andolan (Oscillation)

A controlled wavering between two adjacent notes, creating a rocking motion. This is particularly important in expressing certain ragas' emotional qualities.
